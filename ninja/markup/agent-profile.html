rosetta travel

Rosetta Travel has been in business since 1985.

From the office in Knockbreda Road Belfast, our highly experienced staff have travelled extensively and are skilled to find the right holiday, at a value for money price.

Our areas of speciality include European Sun and Ski, City breaks, USA & Canada, Multi centre Far East, Jersey, Cruising, Escorted tours, Flights ferries & Car hire.

Our regularly updated website www.rosettatravel.com features many unique holidays from Belfast and Dublin. We proactively monitor the market and react quickly when new offers become available. We are financially bonded with ABTA, licensed by IATA, members of Worldchoice, & CLIA. We are agents for most major ATOL protected operators.

Our office hours are Mon to Fri 0930-1730, Sat 1000-1245hrs.
